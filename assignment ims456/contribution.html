Contribution



Among the main contributions and roles of Ungku Aziz to the country are: -

  1. Yang Dipertua, Angkatan Koperasi Kebangsaan Malaysia Berhad (ANGKASA) (March 1971-)

  2. Board Member, Book Store Cooperative of University Malaya Berhad (KKUM) (1968-), (Pekanbuku).

  3. Honorary Fellow, Malaysian Social Institute, Ministry of Women, Family and Community Development (1.10.2005 - 30.9.2008).

  4. Member of the Council of Governance, International Islamic Financial Studies Center, Bank Negara Malaysia (INCEIF Governing Council) (2006-2009).

  5. The implementation of the New Economic Policy (NEP) to help Bumiputera enjoy the wealth distribution of the country and the National Economic Consultative Council (Mapen) II.

  6. The initiative of the establishment of the Board of Directors and Tabung Haji (LTH).

  7. Dewan Bahasa dan Pustaka (DBP).

  8. Founded the University of Malaya.

  9. sparked the idea of the Islamic Academy.

  10. triggered the idea of the Foundation for Science Foundation.

  11. Chairman of Cosmopoint Sdn Bhd (25.9.1995 -).

  12. Director of PLUS Expressways Berhad (11.3.2002 -).

  13. Director of PLUS Berhad (9.9.1988 -).
